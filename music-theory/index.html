Music Theory

“It's easy to play any musical instrument: all you have to do is touch the right key at the right time and the instrument will play itself.” ~ Johann Sebastian Bach

I recommend reading through this guide with a piano in front of you and moving to other instruments once you have a photogenic muscle memory of how music works. I’ll explain how to get to this point too with “Memory Tips” noted down.

The idea is that this guide will give you each piece of the puzzle to:

  • Play music to any song without having to look up sheet music, tabs, chords, etc.
  • Improvise on your favorite instrument.
  • Pick up any instrument and play (piano, guitar, pedal steel, trumpet, clarinet, etc).
