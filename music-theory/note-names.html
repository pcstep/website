Note Names

Start with this: Forget every thing you know about “C” and other lettered notes. Count: 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, and 8. Remember those numbers. Every number represents a note:

  1. Tonic
  2. Super Tonic
  3. Mediant
  4. Subdominant
  5. Dominant
  6. Submediant
  7. Leading
  8. Octave

  • 12:00 PM “Super Tonic (2)”
  • 1:00 PM “Mediant (3)”
  • 2:00 PM “Subdominant (4)”
  • 3:00 PM “Dominant (5)”
  • 4:00 PM “Submediant (6)”
  • 5:00 PM “Leading (7)”
  • 6:00 PM “Octave (8)”
