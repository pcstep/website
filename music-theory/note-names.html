Note Names

Start with this: Forget every thing you know about “C” and other lettered notes. Count: 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, and 8. Remember those numbers. Every number represents a note:

  1. Tonic
  2. Super Tonic
  3. Mediant
  4. Subdominant
  5. Dominant
  6. Submediant
  7. Leading
  8. Octave
